10 Skincare Tips

Sure skin care products are great, however, it is also your skin care habits along side that have a positive impact on your skin. Here are 10 skin care tips you should make a habit of.

1.)Dirty Hands

1.)Dirty Hands

Never touch your face with dirty hands, especially if you are prone to acne. Touching your face with unwashed hands means you can spread bacteria and germs. Touching your pimple with dirty hands can also lead to higher risk of pimples, thus takes longer to heal.

2.)Tap not rub

When applying products to the face,always gently tap the products onto skin until it is no longer sticky and never rub

3.)Sunscreen

3.)Sunscreen

Always apply sunscreen before going outdoors. The sun's UV rays can do more damage to your skin than you can imagine. It can speed up aging, cause pigmentation and cause deep skin cell damage.

4.)Layer your products

When layering products, let each layer sit by tapping gently into the skin before adding the next layer.

5.)Expiry date

Keep track of the expiry date on your products. Products can harbor bacteria over time and produce harmful toxins, thus make your skin prone to infections and irritations.

6.)Face Masks

Make face masks regular. Doing face masks once to three times a week will add immense hydration to your skin

7.) Eat healthy

7.) Eat healthy

Your diet plays a big role in your skin condition and overall health. Avoid consuming excess sugars, and salty foods which can trigger acne.

8.)Abrasive exfoliators

Avoid using abrasive physical scrubs to exfoliate. Instead use chemical exfoliators such as AHA and BHA which protective skin turn over and are much more gentler than physical scrubs which could also be harmful for the environment.
