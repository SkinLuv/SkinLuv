Types of skincare products

Products are essential for maintaining and caring for the skin, However, there are so many different types of of products for the skin, where do you begin? Well, we’ve got you covered! here are the different types of skincare products and their uses.

Cleansers

This can come in the form of oil, foam or liquid. Removes excess oil, dirt make up and ther pollutants from the skin

Toner

Toner is an essential part of the skin care that balances the pH of the skin, gets rid of excess dirt and traces of oil and make up that can could clog pores

Exfoliator

The purpose of exfoliating the skin is to clean out clogged pores and brush off dead skin cells. It is not recommended to exfoliate everyday but rather once a week. Exfoliants that are recommended however are chemical exfoliants eg. products that contain acids such as commonly used AHA (Alpha hydroxy acid) or BHA (beta hydroxy acid).Now you’re probably thinking, there’s no way I’m putting an acid on my skin!, however, using acids are much more gentler than using scrubs and micro beads!

Serums & Ampoules

Serums and ampoules are concentrated treatments that fully moisturise the skin. There are many different types of serums for different purposes such as fading away dark spots, redness, or adding that glow to the skin

Moisturizer

Facial creams are the final step to a skin care routine that is used to lock in the skin’s hydration and all the products added in previous steps to the skin

Sunscreen

The sun can do a surprising amount of damage to the skin even when we don’t notice or feel anything. By forming the habit of applying sunscreen at a younger age, you significantly decrease the rate of aging such as fine lines and age spots and prevent uneven skintone. Applying sunscreen now is something your future self will thank you for.
