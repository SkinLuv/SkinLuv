Oily Skin

Symptoms

  • Large/visible clogged pores
  • Shiny/greasy appearance
  • Prone to whiteheads and blackheads
  • Occasional pimples

Causes

Hormonal changes: Fluctuations our hormones can cause stimulation of sebum production
Poor diet: Eating junk food, sugars can also lead to excess sebum production
Genetics:If your parents have oily skin, it is highly likely you've inherited this trait
Environment: Humidity and hot water stiumulates the secretion of sebum thus leading to the appearance of oily skin

Treatment

  • Avoid pore clogging makeup/ products, or instead, use something more lightweight
  • Use a facial mask once or twice a week
  • Wash your face regularly: Maximum of 2 times a day
  • Moisturize: Although skin is already oily it is still a good idea to moisturize thoroughly. Skipping moisturizer in your routine only calls for more oil to compensate for the lack of moisture.
  • Drink plenty of fluid: It is not only important to moisturize the external surface but it is also important to take into account the hydration internally
