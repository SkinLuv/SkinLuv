Sensitive Skin

Symptoms

  • Prone to rashes and bumps
  • Skin tends to act up with products
  • Itchiness
  • Prone to breakouts

Causes

Hormonal changes: Fluctuations our hormones can cause stimulation of sebum production
Skin conditions

Treatment

  • Use lightweight products on the skin
  • Avoid using products with harsh ingredients: These could irritate your skin even more
  • Shorten your shower use and use warm water rather than hot water
  • Aloe vera gel can be used like a mask to cool and calm the skin
  • Always be sure to wear sunscreen before heading out
  • If problems persist, see a dermatologist
