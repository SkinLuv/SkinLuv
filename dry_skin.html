Dry Skin

Symptoms

  • Tightness after washing/showering
  • Rough Texture
  • Peeling/flaking
  • Redness
  • Itching

Causes

Over Cleansing: You should only be cleansing your face once or twice a day max. Any more than that will strip your skin from natural oils.
Environment: Dry air in he environment or cold conditions such as winter could cause moisture to lift off the skin

Treatment

  • Moiturize your face day and night
  • Use face masks once or twice week
  • Exfoliate once a week: Avoid using facial scrubs which can be way too harsh for our skin. Using acid exfoliators such as AHA and BHA tackles the problem on a microscopic scale.
  • Drink plenty of fluid: It is not only important to moisturize the external surface but it is also important to take into account the hydration internally
